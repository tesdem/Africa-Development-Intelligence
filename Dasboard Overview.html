3. Country Analysis & Policy Simulation
Interactive policy testing laboratory
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  🔍 Country Analysis & Policy Simulation                         │
│  ┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐  │
│  │ [Nigeria ✓]                                               │  │
│  └───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘  │
│                                                                  │
│  Infrastructure: [=====○=====] 1.5x  │  Education: [===○=======] 1.2x  │
│  Trade: [======○====] 1.3x            │
│                                                                  │
│  [🔄 Reset] [📥 Export]                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

🎚️ The Three Policy Levers

  • Infrastructure (0.5x-2.0x): Roads, power, internet, ports
  • Education (0.5x-2.0x): Schools, universities, training
  • Trade (0.5x-2.0x): Exports, imports, trade agreements

📏 What the Multipliers Mean

  • 1.0x: Current spending level
  • 1.5x: 50% increase in investment
  • 0.5x: 50% cut in spending
💡 Real-World Application

Example: Select "Kenya", move Infrastructure to 1.8x, Education to 1.3x → Development index jumps from 0.85 to 1.42, showing infrastructure has highest impact in Kenya.

🎯 Complete User Journey

Just as a pilot uses all instruments together to fly safely, a policymaker uses all dashboard sections together to make informed development decisions. Each section provides a different perspective, and the real power comes from understanding how they interconnect.

👤 User Journey Map

  • 1. Look at metrics: "How is Africa doing overall?"
  • 2. Scan country table: "Which country should I focus on?"
  • 3. Find in bubble chart: "What's their development profile?"
  • 4. Select country: "Let's analyze Kenya specifically"
  • 5. Move sliders: "What if we double infrastructure?"

🎯 Decision Flow

  • 6. Watch policy chart: "How much would that help?"
  • 7. Check importance: "Is infrastructure really the key?"
  • 8. View forecast: "Where will they be in 2030?"
  • 9. Look at network: "Who do they trade with?"
  • 10. Export data: "Save this for my report"
